Educational master in physics

My educational bachelors in physics and chemistry started in 2013, it took me 6 years to finnish both. With those two bachelors im allowed to teach the undergraduate students from age 13 to 16. Since then I have had plenty of experience with this group to understand that I feel most at home with the older students teaching them more complex physics. With these students de curriculum is more context driven. For example: at age 15 I can teach them how household electricity works and how to work on that safely or better jet at age 17 I can teach about de large hadron collider. But at age 13 I am limited to teaching the basics like how to take measurements and what velocity is. In 2021 I decided to enroll in the educational master, a two year program. With this I am allowed to teach the graduate students up to age 19 years old.
